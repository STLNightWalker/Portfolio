My name is Denny. I’m skilled at providing solutions to technical challenges. I have more than a decade of experience in crisis management with little room for error. Now my focus and attention has been to transition into the world of programming. My background, past education, work experience and determination to excel at mastering new technical skills are the components that fuel my drive.
